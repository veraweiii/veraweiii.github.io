Platformer Game

Overview

This platformer game was inspired by fourth graders I tutored who haven't had the opportunity to travel in other areas of the world. Each level of the game features a different cultural scenery. In the future, I hope to add a more educational component to the game, where players can interact with characters and learn fun facts about the corresponding country or culture.

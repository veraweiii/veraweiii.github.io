About

I'm Vera Wei, a multidisciplinary designer, developer, and tinkerer.

As I strongly believe in the power of people-centric design, my problem-solving approach focuses on empathy: mining and understanding latent needs and emotions in the people I design for.

I'm currently getting a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at Carnegie Mellon University. I hope to leverage the synergy of my multidisciplinary background to tackle thorny problems from different perspectives.

Beyond my studies and work, I like to sing (I never say no to karaoke), act (I'm currently in an improv group called "Stalls without Walls"), and travel (my bucket list never ceases to grow). If you're interested in learning more, email me at vera.wei@gmail.com. I'm always open to chat!
